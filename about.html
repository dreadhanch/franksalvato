About Frank

An experienced advocate fighting for your rights

For more than 25 years, Frank Salvato has been fighting to defend the rights of his clients. He has built his reputation on getting results. He is intimately familiar with all facets of the Virginia criminal justice system and has the knowledge and skill to make sure you understand your legal options and to guide you through the legal process.


For more than 25 years, Frank Salvato has represented clients in state and federal courts on charges ranging from minor offenses to serious felonies, including:


  • White Collar Crime
  • Assault and battery
  • DWI/DUI/OWI
  • Drug Offense
  • Theft and larceny
  • Pornography Related Charges
  • Robbery
  • Internet and Computer Crimes
  • Rape
  • Homicide — murder/manslaughter
  • Domestic violence
  • Traffic Offenses

When you have charges on your record that you want eliminated, Frank Salvato can help you get those charges sealed or expunged or represent you in appellate court.


No matter what obstacle has been placed in your way, rest assured that Frank Salvato strives diligently to help you overcome it and works hard to attain the best possible result for you.
