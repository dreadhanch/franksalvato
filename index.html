About Frank

Frank Salvato has won jury trials on charges ranging from DUI to Capital Murder. Over half of the acquittals are against federal prosecutors in Virginia and the District of Columbia.


These not guilty verdicts do not include the multitude of victories in non-jury trials in both state and federal courts, pretrial dismissal of charges, negotiation of favorable plea agreements, departures from sentencing guidelines, successful habeas petitions, expungements, and other noteworthy accomplishments. Such as being featured in ABC's 20/20.


Mr. Salvato has also won cases in the Fourth Circuit Court of Appeals including reversals from trials held before Judge Bryan and Judge Ellis in the Eastern District of Virginia. The reversals resulted in dismissal upon remand.
